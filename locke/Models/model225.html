Model 225 Duplex Reel Mower Attachment

Features

  • Allows Walker Owners/Operators the full benefits of a ride-on reel mower at the cost of an attachment. These benefits include:
  • Innovative design allows for full width mowing.
  • Reel unit shift to tractor width for gate entry and storage.
  • Reel guards allow the options of front throw or rear discharge with simple adjustment.
  • Incorporates Locke's quick adjust height system.( no tools )
  • Attaches easily like walker deck.
  • Saves time! Covers 3 times as much ground as a walk behind reel mower.
  • Full Floating and counterbalanced, reel units do not use caster or rollers in front. (This prevents tracking or disturbing of grass before cutting.)
  • Quick and precise adjustment of reel to bed knife.
  • Fully adjustable spring loaded reel units reduce the possibility of reel damage by passing debris.
  • Uses no hydraulics or external power source.
