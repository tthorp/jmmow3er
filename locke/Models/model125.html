Model 125 Commercial Series

Features

  • A new feature on the commercial series is a "Quick Adjust" height control system that allows, without the use of tools, the cutting height from 1/2" to 3" in 1/4" increments in seconds. This system is linked to a full width aluminum roller that will stripe whether you choose solid traction roller or pneumatic tires.

  • Fully adjustable spring loaded reel unit reduces the possibility of damage to the reel from debris. This also allows for a quick and precise adjustment of the reel to bedknife.

  • Full floating reel assembly does not use casters or rollers in front of the reel to disturb the grass before cutting.

  • All welded rigid steel frame

  • Hardened alloy steel bevel gear differential

  • Dual action roll assembly for superior maneuverability

  • Sealed ball bearings

  • Traction and reel controls on handlebar have operator presence safety feature.

  • Independent clutches for reel and traction drives

  • Shielding over all chain drives

  • Fewer parts than previous models to wear out or replace

  • Ease of service

  • Unequaled balance and handling
